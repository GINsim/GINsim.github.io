A network model for the control of the differentiation process in Th cells. L. Mendoza. Biosystems 84 (2006).
[ doi:10.1016/j.biosystems.2005.10.004 ]

On the basis of an extensive analysis of the literature, L. Mendoza proposed a logical model encompassing the most crucial regulatory components and the cross-interactions involved in these differentiative decisions.

This model encompasses 17 regulatory components.
