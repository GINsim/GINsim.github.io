Hierarchical Transition Graphs

The Hierarchical Transition Graphs (HTG) is an acyclic graph, which provides a compact representation of the State Transition Graph. It improving on the graph of the Strongly Connected Components. by merging linear chains of states (in addition to cycles) into single nodes.

More information on this graph is available in Berenguier et al (2013).

Formats

Like other graphs, HTGs can be saved in the GINML or Z-GINML formats. They can also be exported to the common graph formats.
